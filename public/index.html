    Home

    I’m deeply interested in teachers’ rich experiences during instruction. I study their cognition and emotions in virtual reality settings to scaffold better learning and instruction.

    I worked and studied at the University of Michigan until 2018. I’m now a postdoctoral research scientist and instructor at the University of Potsdam and Kiel University in Germany.
